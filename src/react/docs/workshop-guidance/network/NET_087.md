#  Enable and configure DDoS Alerting, Logging and Metrics

**Implementation Effort:** Medium

**User Impact:** Low

## Overview

Enable and configure DDoS alerting, logging, and metrics to gain actionable insights into volumetric and protocol-based attacks and validate your protection posture. 

First, turn on diagnostic settings for your DDoS Protection Plan (and any protected public IPs) to stream 'DDoSProtectionNotifications', 'DDoSMitigationReports', and `DDoSMitigationFlowLogs` logs into a Log Analytics workspace or Event Hub. Next, configure Azure Monitor metric alerts on key metricsâ€”such as `IfUnderDDoSAttack` to notify your security teams or trigger automated playbooks when DDoS events occur.
